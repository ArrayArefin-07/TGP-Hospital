Your Health is Our Priority

At TGP Hospital, we provide comprehensive healthcare services with state-of-the-art technology and a team of experienced medical professionals dedicated to your well-being.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of medical services to meet all your healthcare needs.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care with advanced diagnostic tools and treatment options.

Pediatrics

Specialized care for children from infancy through adolescence.

Neurology

Expert diagnosis and treatment for disorders of the nervous system.

Orthopedics

Treatment for musculoskeletal injuries and conditions with cutting-edge techniques.

Pulmonology

Specialized care for respiratory system disorders including asthma, COPD, and lung diseases.

Internal Medicine

Comprehensive care for adults, focusing on the prevention and treatment of adult diseases.

Dentistry

Complete oral health care including preventive, restorative, and cosmetic dentistry.

Ophthalmology

Expert eye care services from routine vision exams to advanced surgical procedures.

Dermatology

Diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions, from acne to skin cancer detection.

Psychiatry

Mental health care including diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental disorders.

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Comprehensive women's health services including prenatal care, delivery, and reproductive health.

Radiology

Advanced imaging services including MRI, CT scans, ultrasound, and X-ray for accurate diagnosis.

Oncology

Comprehensive cancer care including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical oncology.

Gastroenterology

Specialized care for digestive system disorders including endoscopy and colonoscopy services.

ENT (Otolaryngology)

Treatment for ear, nose, and throat disorders, including hearing and sinus problems.

Nephrology

Specialized care for kidney diseases, dialysis, and hypertension management.

Hematology

Diagnosis and treatment of blood disorders, including anemia and clotting problems.

Endocrinology

Management of hormonal disorders including diabetes, thyroid issues, and metabolic conditions.

Physical Therapy

Rehabilitation services to restore movement and function following injury or illness.

Emergency Medicine

24/7 emergency care for acute illnesses and injuries with a dedicated trauma team.

About TGP Hospital

TGP Hospital has been serving the community for over 25 years, providing exceptional healthcare with compassion and innovation. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest medical technology, and our team of board-certified physicians and healthcare professionals are dedicated to delivering the highest standard of care.

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the illness, and we're committed to making a positive difference in the lives of our patients and their families.
