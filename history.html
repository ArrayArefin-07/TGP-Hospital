Our Journey Through Time

For over 25 years, TGP Hospital has been at the forefront of healthcare innovation and patient care.

1998

The Foundation

TGP Hospital was founded with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to the community. We started as a small clinic with just 10 beds and a dedicated team of 5 physicians.

2005

First Expansion

We expanded our facilities to include a new wing with state-of-the-art operation theaters and increased our capacity to 50 beds. This allowed us to serve more patients and introduce specialized departments.

2010

Technology Advancement

TGP Hospital became the first in the region to implement electronic health records and telemedicine capabilities, revolutionizing patient care and accessibility.

2015

Research Center Establishment

We established our Research and Innovation Center, focusing on medical breakthroughs and training the next generation of healthcare professionals.

2020

COVID-19 Response

During the global pandemic, TGP Hospital served as a regional center for COVID-19 treatment, implementing innovative protocols and treatments that saved countless lives.

2023

New Cancer Center

We opened our state-of-the-art Cancer Treatment Center, offering comprehensive oncology services with the latest technology and compassionate care.
