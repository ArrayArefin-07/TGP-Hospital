Our Mission

Our Journey

TGP Hospital was established in 1998 with a vision to provide world-class healthcare services to the community. What started as a modest 50-bed facility has now grown into a 300-bed multi-specialty hospital with state-of-the-art technology and a team of highly skilled medical professionals.

Over the years, we have consistently expanded our services, incorporating the latest medical advancements while maintaining our core values of compassion, integrity, and excellence in patient care.

Today, TGP Hospital stands as a testament to our commitment to healthcare excellence, serving thousands of patients annually with a comprehensive range of medical services.
