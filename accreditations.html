Accreditations & Certifications

Demonstrating our commitment to excellence in healthcare through rigorous quality standards

Our Commitment to Quality

TGP Hospital has earned numerous accreditations and certifications from leading healthcare organizations worldwide. These recognitions demonstrate our unwavering commitment to providing the highest standard of patient care, safety, and medical excellence.

Joint Commission International

JCI accreditation is the gold standard in global healthcare. We have maintained this prestigious accreditation since 2015, demonstrating our commitment to the highest international healthcare standards.

Accredited Since: 2015 Renewed: 2023

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

Our quality management system is certified to ISO 9001:2015 standards, ensuring consistent delivery of high-quality healthcare services and continuous improvement in all our processes.

Certified Since: 2012 Renewed: 2024

NABH Accreditation

We are accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH), India's highest standard for hospital quality and patient safety.

Accredited Since: 2018 Renewed: 2022

CAP Accreditation

Our laboratory is accredited by the College of American Pathologists (CAP), meeting the highest standards of excellence in laboratory medicine and pathology services.

Accredited Since: 2016 Renewed: 2023

AAHRPP Accreditation

We have earned full accreditation from the Association for the Accreditation of Human Research Protection Programs (AAHRPP), demonstrating our commitment to ethical research practices.

Accredited Since: 2019 Renewed: 2023

Baby-Friendly Hospital Initiative

We are designated as a Baby-Friendly hospital by WHO and UNICEF, recognizing our commitment to optimal infant feeding practices and mother-baby bonding.

Designated Since: 2020 Renewed: 2023
